The Spark of an Idea

From a Dark Room to a Bright Idea!

How 'Light It Up!' was born from a power outage!

Imagine this: a cozy evening, then BAM! Everything goes dark. But in that darkness, a brilliant idea sparked! What if we could bring light back, one wire at a time? That's how 'Light It Up!' was created - a game where you're the hero who brings light to the neighborhood!

Let's light up the world!
